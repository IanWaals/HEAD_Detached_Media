Lena Voss is 35, she has a degree in business and computer science from the university of Shelby, the largest school in the united states. She now holds a powerful position at Vireon being one of the most influential people in the company. She is the supervisor for the entire software department and on the board of directors with all the other department leaders and the CEO, [[Silas Kane]]. Unlike [[Eli Mercer]], Lena grew up in a wealthy family, not millionaires, but any ‘regular’ person would feel envy walking past her childhood home. Her dad was in the police force and she was spoiled all throughout her childhood. She hasn’t ever faced real consequences despite having committed some minor offenses, none big enough to face time in prison but not something you would want on your personal record. She was hired as Senior Developer at Vireon at 28 years old, and she quickly impressed the companies leaders with her innate ability to come up with new technologies, usually very complicated, and very lucrative for Vireon, which quickly moved Lena into the position she’s in now.
